Grateful for an MPA

Gaining Valuable Insight

I am so grateful for the opportunity I have had to work on my MPA at BYU. I have been blessed to learn that we not only desire a job we enjoy, but we must also seek to become better each day.

I have worked as a student equipment manager for BYU Football and it is my dream job. I have gained so much experience from assisting with uniforms for marketing and photo shoots to ensuring equipment is ready for the players at game time. I am currently a graduate assistant and would love the opportunity to keep doing something I love.

Learning about service in the MPA Program has helped me see the role it can have in sports. No matter what a person's background may be, they can find common ground in sports. I want to serve and unite others through sports. I love how excited people get cheering for their team and I want that excitement to continue off of the field to help us become better.
